App will be made with a React frontend and a Node.js backend using GraphQl and MongoDB.

App will have discussion, with each discussion containing user made posts which can be commented upon by other users 
and these comments, in turn, can be commented by other users. Each post and comment can be liked or disliked.
Users can join or leave discussions as they wish, each discussion keeps tally of members. Each user has total likes and dislikes,
which increment as their posts or comments get liked or disliked.
